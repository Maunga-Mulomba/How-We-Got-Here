How We Got Here

This project is based on two companion books, told through the eyes of two fictional narrators living in distant cities. Each reflects on what it felt like to watch their world change as artificial intelligence became part of everyday life.

The two cities took opposite paths in how they developed and governed AI. One kept it as a tool and the other let it grow beyond control.
